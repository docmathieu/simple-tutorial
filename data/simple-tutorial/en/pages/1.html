Introduction

Welcome to the tutorial of Simple Tutorial !
This tutorial explain how to create a simple container for tutorials on your server.
After doing the requirements, you should complete this tutorial in 20 minutes.


Requirements

You must have:

  • LAMP or WAMP server, rewrite module activated.
  • Sources of Simple Tutorial, available on GitHub: https://github.com/docmathieu/simple-tutorial
  • Your hosts and virtual hosts configured with ServerName like tuto.mywebsite.com


Use Simple Tutorial:

The horizontal bar at the top contains two elements:

  • 3 bars representing a button to open the menu, you can also open the page by moving the mouse to the left of the page.
  • The title of the tutorial, if you click it you will return to the list of your tutorials.


At the bottom right of the page a link provides access to the next chapter, click on it.
